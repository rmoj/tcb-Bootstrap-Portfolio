About Me

I subscribe to three basic principles - understand what's to be done before doing it, use the right tools for the job, and make it work, make it right, THEN make it fast. A problem solver with a new found passion for learning and using new technologies to create applications with aesthetic, intuitive interfaces. A team player with time management skills who can adapt to change. What fascinates me is the way technology affects our lives and how best to use it to improve on how we do things. I love hiking, the outdoors, martial arts, and watching films.
